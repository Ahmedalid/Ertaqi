who are we?

Our idea was launched in response to the need of those wishing to learn the Arabic language and understand its branches in the absence of a training program that addresses expatriates and non-native speakers and takes into account their environment and circumstances.

Disover Ertaqi
Let's Learn Together

Therefore, a group of qualified specialists created a specialized training program that teaches reading and writing the Arabic language within a period of three months, in addition to designing an advanced training program for the professionalization of articulation at its various levels based on the applied understanding of Arabic grammar.

Innovative Learning

The application and teaching of our educational programs have been started since 2015, and since that time they have been continuously developed, with a success efficiency of 98%, and with the expansion of the number of those wishing to join our programs, an interactive electronic platform has been created that presents our programs in an innovative way to those interested around the world. .

Our goals

1- Working to spread the Arabic language and its branches around the world, in a simple and enjoyable way far from complexity. 2- Teaching the Arabic language to Arabs abroad to master reading, speaking and thinking. 3- Teaching the rules of recitation and recitation of the Noble Qur’an. 4- Providing an innovative interactive platform for those wishing to present their scientific content to the public.

Our Vision

The most prominent platform in teaching Arabic and its branches, for expatriates and non-native speakers around the world.
